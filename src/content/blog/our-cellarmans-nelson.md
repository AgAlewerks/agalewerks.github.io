We did a single hop IPA, using the [Martin House formula](http://martinhousebrewing.com/the-beer/cellarmans-reserve-ipa/), with [Nelson Sauvin hops](https://ychhops.com/varieties/nelson-sauvin).

Very simple grist:

1. 88% two row barley
2. 12% light Munich

Straightforward hop schedule:

1. 65 IBU worth in the boil at 60 minutes
2. At 5 minutes add 0.2 oz/gallon
3. Dry hop with 0.2 oz/gallon

Ferment (we used a [Vermont Ale strain](http://www.theyeastbay.com/brewers-yeast-products/vermont-ale)), cold-crash with gelatin, keg, carbonate, and enjoy.
